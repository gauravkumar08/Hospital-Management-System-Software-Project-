Overview


The hospital has various departments offering healthcare services in specific areas. All clinical departments treat their outdoor patients in the OPD. Indoor patients are treated in wards, generally organised according to specialties. The 100+ year old hospital offers care and cure facilities through its OPD & dispensaries, indoor medical and surgical departments, 24-hour emergency and casualty management facilities, diagnostics and critical care services. The hospital also maintains a medical store for procuring and storing medicines, implants and all consumables. The medicines are dispensed to patients through pharmacy counters located in the OPD complex and various dispensaries. For administering inpatients, wards requisition their requirement of medicines to the medical stores.
