Ambulance Services


The ambulance service is mainly to bring patients from their residence and drop them after discharge. For discharged patients, the ambulance service is available from 12.30 PM to 1:30 PM, 2.00 PM to 3.00 PM and from 6.00 PM to 7:00 PM . For non-entitled patients, a fee is charged for a single trip.




Hearse Services


The hearse service is provided to transport bodies of the deceased within the jurisdiction of JNAC from 6:30 PM to 9:30 PM. The service is free for Tata Steel employees and dependant relations.


For non-entitled patients, a fee is charged for a single trip.
